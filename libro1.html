Why choose this book?

The book serves students, researchers, and instructors who need straightforward procedures for quantitative studies in second language education.

Wide coverage

From descriptive statistics to regression, ANOVA families, non-parametric tests, effect sizes, and a concise start in Bayesian analysis.
